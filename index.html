    Since 2002, Linkou Chang Gung Memorial Hospital has been cooperating with the National Health Insurance Administration of the Ministry of Health and Welfare to implement the "Taoyuan City Fuxing District Medical Reimbursement Efficiency Improvement Project". The "Hualing Medical Station" was established in Hualing Village, Fuxing District, which lacks medical resources. The station provides medical care to about 3,000 residents in Hualing Village, Sanguang Village, and Gaoyi Village in the back mountain area. It offers three outpatient clinics, 24-hour emergency services, and mobile medical services to villages every day.
